Pune Transportation: A Comprehensive Overview

Pune, a bustling metropolis in India, offers a diverse range of transportation options to cater to the needs of its residents and visitors. From public transit to private vehicles, the city has something to offer everyone.

Pune Mahanagar Parivahan Mahamandal Limited (PMPML)

The backbone of public transportation in Pune, PMPML operates an extensive network of buses across the city. These buses provide a cost-effective way to travel within Pune.

Pune Suburban Railway

Connecting Pune to Lonavala, this suburban rail service offers a convenient and faster alternative to road travel for commuters between these two cities.

Pune Metro

The Pune Metro is a rapid transit system under construction, with several lines planned. It is expected to significantly improve the city's transportation infrastructure.

Auto-rickshaws

A popular mode of transport for short distances, auto-rickshaws are readily available throughout the city.
