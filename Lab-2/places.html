Pune: A Treasure Trove of History

Pune, often referred to as the 'Oxford of the East', boasts a rich historical heritage. Its legacy is etched in the numerous forts, palaces, and temples that dot the city and its surroundings.

Forts

Sihagad Fort

A majestic hill fort with a glorious history, Sinhagad offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.

Shivneri Fort

The birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, this fort holds immense historical importance.

Places

Shaniwar Wada

The former residence of the Peshwa rulers, this magnificent palace is a symbol of Maratha power.

Aga Khan Palace

Known for its association with Mahatma Gandhi, this palace is now a historical monument.
